Who We Are

Guided by Legacy.
Driven by Architecture.

D Al Tamimi Capital serves as the principal investment and structuring platform of a privately held Gulf-based family office, operating at the intersection of capital architecture, institutional continuity, and nationally aligned innovation. With deep regional roots and a long-term orientation, we engage selectively in initiatives that strengthen the region's position as a global destination for capital, enterprise, and talent.

Our activities include designing and structuring institutional-grade foreign direct investment platforms; developing intergenerational governance and continuity frameworks for family enterprises; and originating select technology ventures aligned with national economic priorities.

Across all initiatives, we prioritise scale, governance, and long-term alignment, working alongside global capital partners and Tier-1 operating counterparts.
